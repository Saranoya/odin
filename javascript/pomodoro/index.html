The Pomodoro Technique

  1. Pick a task you want to accomplish today.
  2. Work on that task in a focused manner for a fixed period of time.
  3. Take a short break.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until you have completed two or more focused work sessions or 'pomodoros'.
  5. Take a longer break.
  6. Repeat steps 1 through 5 until your task is complete, or your workday is over.
